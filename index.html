    campus life

    School Library

    The school library has a collection of 13000 books other than reference books. This includes Encyclopedias, Quiz Books, G.K Books, Books on Fiction. We subscribe to about 35 periodicals. For each subject we have reference books. Students from Class I to XII have a weekly library period to inculcate the habit of reading. Books are issued to the students from Class V to Class XII. Apart from books we have Audio-Visual equipment with CD’S on different subjects and also on Fiction.

    Digital Learning

    The school has audio visual aids like CD Players, LCD's, VCR, OHP, K-YAN & Multi Media and latest educational CDs to impart education effectively. The Digital smartboards are in the offing, adding a new dimension to the Information and Communication Technology.

    Cafeteria

    Keeping in mind the nutritional needs and tastes of the students, the school gives a planned menu to the cafeteria management which is viewed from time-to-time by the school interms of quality and maintaining hygienic standards.

    School Achivements

    VIRTUAL RENDEZVOUS WITH Ms IBU CARMANITA

    SThe school planned a Virtual Interactive session with a fashion designer Ms Ibu Carmanita from Indonesia on the 10 December, 2020.She gave us a comparative study of Ikat in Indonesia and India. With the support and motivation of the Management of DDMS P OBUL REDDY PUBLIC SCHOOL, the school initiated the process to instill a global dimension into the learning experience and registered for the British Council International School Award (ISA).

    A meet with Padmashri Sri Chintakindi Mallesham

    The school organised a webinar on Ikat, as part of its ISA Programme, Ikat- The Threads that Blur and Blend. This project involves learning through interaction with various people associated with Ikat. This webinar was organised on 21 December, and the Resource Person was none other than Padmashri Sri Chintakindi Mallesham, who brought in a revolution in Ikat Weaving Technology.
